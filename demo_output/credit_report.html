Glossary & How to read this report

Score: Numeric value representing the bureau's assessment of risk (higher = lower risk).

Utilisation: Total revolving balances divided by total revolving limits (expressed as %).

Payment history: A 24-month grid showing on-time/late payments for each account. Repeated late payments and missed payments increase risk.

Public records: County Court Judgements (CCJs), bankruptcy orders, Individual Voluntary Arrangements (IVAs) or similar legal filings.
